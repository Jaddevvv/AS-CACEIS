  KPI 1: HCVA / HCROI Per FTE

  Goal:

  Prove whether employees are generating increasing financial value.

  Formulas:

  HCVA = PNB - Other Operating Costs

  HCVA per FTE = HCVA / Average FTE

  HCROI = (PNB - Other Operating Costs) / Total Personnel Costs

  Revenue per FTE = PNB / Average FTE

  Work to do:

  | Step | Task |
  |---:|---|
  | 1 | Extract yearly P&L rows from Synthese_PL |
  | 2 | Extract yearly average FTE from Synthese_ETP |
  | 3 | Build yearly finance KPI table, 2022-2025 |
  | 4 | Plot HCVA per FTE, HCROI, Revenue per FTE |
  | 5 | Add business interpretation: value creation vs workforce cost |

  KPI 2: Workforce Value-at-Risk

  Goal:

  Identify employees or segments where CACEIS risks losing human capital value.

  Target variable:

  Left_Next_6M = 1 if employee disappears from the monthly panel for the following 6 months
  Left_Next_6M = 0 otherwise

  Features:

  Tenure_CACEIS_Months = PERIOD - DATE_ENTRY_CACEIS

  Months_In_Current_Post = PERIOD - DATE_ENTRY_POSTE

  Absence_Days_12M = sum absence days per employee over last 12 months

  Training_Hours_12M = sum training hours per employee over last 12 months

  Performance_Score = EAE score

  Simple risk score:

  Risk Score =
  0.25 * New Joiner Risk
  + 0.20 * Low Performance Risk
  + 0.20 * High Absence Risk
  + 0.15 * Low Training Risk
  + 0.10 * Long Time In Post Risk
  + 0.10 * Segment Risk

  Work to do:

  | Step | Task |
  |---:|---|
  | 1 | Build employee-month table from Data.xlsx > Sheet1 |
  | 2 | Detect employees who disappear from the panel |
  | 3 | Join EAE, training, and absence features |
  | 4 | Create risk score |
  | 5 | Show top-risk entities/jobs/employee segments |

  KPI 3: Learning-to-Performance Index

  Goal:

  Show whether training investment improves capability and performance.

  Formulas:

  Training_Hours = SUM(Total_Training_Hours)

  Training_Count = COUNT(Session_ID)

  Quick_Review_Score = AVG(Note générale)

  Cold_Impact_Score = average converted cold-review answers

  Cold review scoring:

  Oui, tout à fait = 1
  Oui, en partie = 0.5
  Non = 0

  Main KPI:

  Learning-to-Performance Index =
  0.40 * Normalized Training Hours
  + 0.30 * Normalized Cold Impact Score
  + 0.20 * Normalized Quick Review Score
  + 0.10 * Certification Flag

  Work to do:

  | Step | Task |
  |---:|---|
  | 1 | Aggregate training records by employee-year |
  | 2 | Aggregate quick and cold review scores |
  | 3 | Join with EAE performance scores |
  | 4 | Compare trained vs non-trained employees |
  | 5 | Plot training hours vs performance / impact score |

  KPI 4: Absence Productivity Drag

  Goal:

  Translate absenteeism into lost productive capacity and estimated value loss.

  Formulas:

  Total Absence Days = SUM(Jours Ouvrés Absence)

  Lost FTE = Total Absence Days / 220

  Absence Days per Employee = Total Absence Days / Number of Employees

  Absence Productivity Drag = Lost FTE / Average FTE

  Estimated Value Lost = Lost FTE * HCVA per FTE

  Work to do:

  | Step | Task |
  |---:|---|
  | 1 | Load absence files for 2023, 2024, 2025 |
  | 2 | Standardize employee ID, month, reason, absence days |
  | 3 | Aggregate by month, entity, reason, employee |
  | 4 | Convert absence days into lost FTE |
  | 5 | Multiply lost FTE by HCVA per FTE to estimate value loss |

  KPI 5: Role Cost Efficiency Index

  Goal:

  Check if role compensation cost is aligned with performance.

  Important limitation:

  Compensation is job-level average, not employee salary.
  Do not claim individual salary analysis.

  Formulas:

  Average Total Compensation = Average Fixed Salary + Average Variable Salary

  Variable Pay Ratio = Average Variable Salary / Average Total Compensation

  Role Compensation Index =
  Average Total Compensation / Median Average Total Compensation in same country-year

  Average Role Performance = AVG(EAE Performance Score) by job/year/country

  Role Cost Efficiency =
  Normalized Average Role Performance / Role Compensation Index

  Quadrants:

  | Segment | Meaning |
  |---|---|
  | High cost + high performance | Strategic high-value role |
  | High cost + low performance | Efficiency risk |
  | Low cost + high performance | Retention risk |
  | Low cost + low performance | Low-impact role |

  Work to do:

  | 1 | Transform compensation FR/LU sheets into clean long format |
  | 2 | Join compensation benchmark to employee job/country/year |
  | 3 | Aggregate EAE performance by job/country/year |
  | 4 | Calculate role compensation index |
  | 5 | Build cost-performance quadrant chart |
